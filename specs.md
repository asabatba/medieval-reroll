
specs

- the goal is to generate people that follow realistic lives according to their era

- ideally there would be no "simulation", everything would be computed in a similar way to the online library of babel

- major events would be rooted in reality

- jobs, death causes and life expectancy, etc, would be rooted in reality and available statistics

- the people and events would be consistent

- you should be able to jump from a person to another related person (parents, spouse, children, etc)

- migration should be factored in

- easily expandable with new names, surnames, places, etc
